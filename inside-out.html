CBT Thought Check
Challenge unhelpful thoughts and find balanced ones — step by step
Step 1
🌩️ What is the worry or difficult thought?
Write out the thought exactly as it feels. Don't judge it — just capture it.
Step 2
🔍 Is there a thinking trap? (Cognitive Distortion)
Does this thought match any of these common thinking traps? Tap any that apply:
Step 3
📋 What is the evidence FOR this thought?
What facts or experiences make you believe this thought might be true?
Step 4
🌤️ What is the evidence AGAINST this thought?
What facts or experiences suggest this thought might not be completely true?
Step 5
💡 What else could be true? (Balanced Thought)
Write a more balanced, helpful thought that takes ALL the evidence into account.
Step 6
⭐ What is one helpful action I can take?
Based on your balanced thought, what is one small, positive thing you can do?
Therapist guide: Work through the steps together. You lead Steps 2–4 (evidence gathering) while your client drives Steps 1, 5, and 6. Validate before challenging — "That makes sense you feel that way, AND let's look at all the evidence."
